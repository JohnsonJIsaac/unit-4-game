Quest for Morpheus' Relics

You will be given a dream number at the start of the game.


There are four relics below. By clicking on a relic you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the dream number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the dream number.


The value of each relic is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each relic.
